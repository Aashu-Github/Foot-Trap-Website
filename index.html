Spain Pathway

Train & trial in Europe.

FootTrap connects players to professional environments in Spain and across Europe through our network of clubs, sporting directors, and coaches.

The Spain Pathway gives qualified players access to training camps and real club tryout opportunities abroad. This isn't tourism — it's structured exposure to a professional environment, assessed by people inside the game.

Players who go through the pathway receive a pre-trip evaluation, in-camp assessment, and a written post-camp report they can share with coaches back home.

About FootTrap

Built on film, not flattery.

FootTrap Soccer Consulting works directly with players and families to cut through recruiting noise. Every evaluation is grounded in match footage and tactical detail — not generic praise.

We're based in Frisco, TX and serve clients across the DFW area in person and anywhere in the country remotely. Our network includes coaches, scouts, analysts, sporting directors, and club managers at every level.

The goal is simple: give every player an honest, professional read on where they stand and a concrete plan to get where they're going. Parents get educated, not just updated.
